BEST GAME OF 2018, FORTNITE

2012

The next year, Chinese gaming giant Tencent acquired a huge stake in Epic, and the company’s development focus began to shift to reflect their new investor’s free-to-play priorities.

That same year, Cliff Bleszinski left Epic, as did designer Rob Ferguson. Cliff eventually moved on to Lawbreakers and Radical Heights, while Ferguson became a key figure in the next-gen rebirth of Gears of War.

2013

That same year, Cliff Bleszinski left Epic, as did designer Rob Ferguson. Cliff eventually moved on to Lawbreakers and Radical Heights, while Ferguson became a key figure in the next-gen rebirth of Gears of War. In 2013, Epic and co-developer People Can Fly completed Gears of War: Judgement, the last traditional console shooter created by Epic. Development on Fortnite continued behind the scenes.

2014

In 2014, Game Informer revealed the long-awaited secrets of Epic’s new project in their Fortnite: Save the World cover story and supplemental media. The systems of base building and scavenging were clearly established, as were the game’s focus on terrain conquering and cooperation. The media drop highlighted class-based gameplay and Fortnite’s free-to-play structure. A few months later, closed alpha testing began.
