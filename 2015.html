BEST GAME OF 2018, FORTNITE

2015-2016

For two years, Fortnite went relatively quiet. In 2015, Mac compatibility was revealed. Testing continued in the background, shifting from alpha to beta, and every now and then Fortnite made a press appearance, but there’s wasn’t a lot happening in the limelight.

2017

In mid-2017, Fortnite kicked into high gear. In June, Epic began offering Fortnite Founders Packs for Save the World paid early access.

While Fortnite would eventually release as a free-to-play game, players who purchased Founders Packs joined the extensive early access period as well as reaping other bonuses.

In July, Save the World early access became available online for PC, Mac, Xbox One, and PS4.

In September 2017, Epic formally revealed Fortnite Battle Royale. Inspired by PUBG, Battle Royale was initially revealed as a separate mode within paid early access Save The World, but Battle Royale was quickly redefined as a distinct product free to everyone. Battle Royale featured no paid gameplay enhancements and a cosmetic-based FTP revenue model. Fortnite BR was released in late September on PC, Mac, Xbox One, and PS4.

In October, IGN released its review of Fortnite: Save the World early access, giving it a score of 6.5.
